01. Obituary Website

Internship Project - Aadhira Holdings (Pvt) Ltd

Role: Project Manager & UI/UX Contributor

Technologies: MERN Stack (Express.js, React.js, Node.js) + MySQL

Tools: Figma, Miro, Clickup, VS code

As the Project Manager, I led a cross-functional team to design and develop a compassionate and user-friendly Obituary Website. The platform allows users to publish and share tributes for their loved ones in a respectful digital space.

While overseeing the project lifecycle-from planning to deployment - I also played an active role in UI/UX design, frontend development, and team collaboration.

Key Features:

  • Create and manage obituary notices with images, personal messages, and funeral details
  • Search and filter obituaries by name and date
  • Comment sections for condolences and tributes
  • Social sharing features for broader community engagement

Highlights:

  • Designed user flows, wireframes, and prototypes in Figma to ensure an intuitive and accessible interface
  • Used Miro to map user journeys, brainstorm features, and align the team on key decisions
  • Focused on clean design, empathetic tone, and mobile responsiveness for ease of use across all devices

Website Features:

  • Post obituary notices with photos, messages, and funeral details
  • Search and filter obituaries by name/date
  • Allow users to post condolences or tributes
  • Share obituary pages through social media

This project was a meaningful opportunity that allowed me to grow as a project leader while sharpening my skills in user-centered design, design thinking, cross-functional teamwork, and full-stack development. It blended empathy, design, and technology to serve a real-world need, and helped me gain valuable experience in leading with purpose while actively contributing to both the creative and technical aspects of the solution.
