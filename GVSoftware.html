Green Valley Resort Wedding Package

Technologies used: React.js, Tailwind CSS, Vite, Vercel

Green Valley Resort offers an exquisite wedding experience in a breathtaking natural setting. Our wedding package web application provides couples with an intuitive platform to explore venue options, customize their wedding package, and manage all details of their special day. The responsive design ensures a seamless experience across all devices, allowing couples to plan their perfect wedding from anywhere.

Key Features:

  • Customizable wedding packages
  • Venue gallery and virtual tours
  • Guest list and RSVP management
  • Budget calculator and payment tracking
